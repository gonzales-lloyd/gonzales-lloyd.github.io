In a recursion-tree method, we attempt to convert the recurrence into a tree, where each node represents the cost incurred at that level of recursion, excluding the cost of recursive calls. We then sum up the costs of all levels.

The goal here is to get a potential "guess" for the recurrence when we perform the substitution method. Sometimes, you may get a tight bound when using the recursion method, but often you will need to use the substitution method.
